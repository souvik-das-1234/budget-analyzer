Launching Your Influencer Program

An influencer program should not be measured by the same CPL as your ads. Its value lies in brand building, trust, and authentic reach. Focus on these keys to success.

Track the Right Metrics
  • Brand Awareness: Reach & Impressions
  • Audience Growth: New Followers
  • Engagement: Likes, Comments, Shares
  • Conversions: Use unique discount codes or affiliate links
Start Small & Test

Use the initial ₹18,000 to collaborate with a few relevant micro-influencers. Analyze what content and which creators resonate most effectively with your target audience before scaling up.

Think Long-Term

Building authentic relationships with creators and seeing a significant return from influencer marketing is a marathon, not a sprint. Consistency is key.
